About Gina




In my current position as the Digital Graphics Coordinator for Faber-Castell USA, I utilize my skill set in cross media to enhance our website and our online presence with social media using images and videos. In December 2014, I obtained a Bachelor of Science in Technology Degree in Visual Communication Technology with a double specialization in Web Design & Video Production. The program allowed me to explore different additional cross-media outlets, such as photography, print media, and social media.

In my spare time, I run an Etsy shop where I paint canvases. It's called Razzy Custom Creations. I also dabble with the guitar, piano, ukulele, and harmonica. I love Katy Perry, TLC's "Friday Bride Day," the Cleveland Indians and Chicago Cubs.
